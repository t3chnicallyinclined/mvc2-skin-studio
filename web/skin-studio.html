How it works
  1. The page auto-loads your character data (decoded once by python tools/build_skin_studio_data.py <track03.bin>). Pick a character, recolor swatches, and/or pick an animation → step frames → paint.
  2. Hit ⬇ bake to ROM. With skin_server.py running it patches track03.bin in place (a one-time pristine track03.bin.bak is made by the extract step, so you can always undo).
  3. Load the .gdi in flycast — or burn / GDEMU for real Dreamcast.
Serve over http://localhost. Chrome/Edge can also bake without the server: click 📂 load track03.bin, then ⬇ bake to ROM (writes in place via the File System Access API). Nothing is ever uploaded.
Export frames & animations
  1. ⤓ frame — export the current assembled frame as a PNG.
  2. ⤓ animation — export the whole current animation as one sprite-sheet PNG (every frame, aligned to a shared box).
Great for reference, sharing, or opening in Aseprite/GIMP. You edit the sprite itself right here — paint on the frame and recolor the palette, then bake.
